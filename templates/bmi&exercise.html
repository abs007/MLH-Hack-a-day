EXERCISES

PUSH UP

Builds upper body strength, and also strengthen the lower back and core.

SIDE LUNGES

Develop balance, stability, and strength. Strengthens inner and outer thighs.

TRICEP DIPS

Strengthen the upper body (triceps, shoulders, and the arms), and increase the range of motion of the body.

JUMPING JACKS

Works out all major muscles in the lower body, strengthening the glutes, hamstrings, quads, hips, calves, and shins.

CRUNCHES

Strengthening the core muscles, improving posture, and increasing the mobility and flexibility of the muscles.

SUMO SQUAT

Activates muscle groups throughout your lower body, including your quadriceps, hamstrings, hip flexors, glutes, calves, and lower-back muscles.

QUAD STRETCH

Activates the muscles, stimulates blood flow, and can increase your short-term range of motion and flexibility.

SQUATS

Lowers chance of injuring your knees and ankles. Strenghthens tendons, bones, and ligament around the leg muscles.

BUTT KICKS

Gains better form, efficiency, and protection from injury.
